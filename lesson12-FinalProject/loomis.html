Events in Loomis

Loomis Christmas Gift Market

Sun Dec 19, 10 AM - 4 PM @ Blue Goose Event Center

3550 Taylor Rd, Loomis, CA

Find gifts for your family and friends at the Loomis Christmas Gift Market! Over 30 local vendors will offer their wares inside the charming Blue Goose Event Center.

BINGO Round-Up

Wed Apr 13, 11 AM - 8:30 PM @ Blue Goose Event Center

3550 Taylor Rd, Loomis, CA

Round up your friends and attend their 12th annual Bingo Round-Up. This event is a signature fundraiser for the nonprofit, all-volunteer organization that is celebrating over 30 years of service in the Greater Placer community.

Storytime Fridays - Outside on the lawn.

Fri May 13, 10:30 - 11 AM @ Loomis Library & Community Learning Center

6050 Library Dr, Loomis, CA

Children ages 0-5 are welcome to attend on Friday morning's to explore the wonders of books and the thrill of reading. Our storytime readers will share exciting stories from notable children's books, singing, movement activities, flannel board fun, and more!
