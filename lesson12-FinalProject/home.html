LFB's Purpose and Goals

The Loomis Family Business is a chamber of commerce dedicated to helping businesses in our small town of Loomis grow. No matter how small, we will do our best to promote and spread the news of your company and bring you the customers that you deserve.

Goals

  1. Increase the number of custombers.
  2. Inform those in Loomis of your business.
  3. Bring the businesses of Loomis closer together.
