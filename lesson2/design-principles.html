Design Principles Document

Lanea C. Medlar

Alignment

Webtoons

webtoons.com

Webtoons is a collection of online comics that not only uses this principle, but depends on it for its website to look like it is. The comics are organized in these little squares, almost like a quilt before your stitch it together. Each one is lined up with its neighbor, resulting in a very clean look that is organized and polished.

White Space and Clean Design

Oculus

oculus.com

Oculus designed their page with plenty of white space in between each set of information, allowing the website to breath. Each section is space apart and given a gap. The design is also very clean and straighforward, little sections telling the audience about why their product is so great and worth buying. Everything is aligned and sharp.

Contrast

Nintendo

nintendo.com

Nintendo has a signiture color, red. They use this very well in their website design by highlighting the most important information and buttons with that bright color. They use it against a white background, allowing for the color to further pop. They also use size to contrast between what they deem more important for the viewer to see, making their different sections vary in size.
